Who we are

We are Atmar Bio

We develop and supply flavours for food, beverage, pet food, and feed producers across Europe and beyond. Our founders are food scientists, and we built this company the way we wanted to be treated as customers: clear answers, fair prices, and samples that actually arrive when promised.

Since 2018 we have worked with brand owners, private-label manufacturers, and contract producers in dairy, bakery, confectionery, beverage, alcoholic drinks, and animal nutrition. The brief is always different. The process is always the same: understand what you are making, match the flavour that fits, document it properly, and deliver on time.

What we do

Four services, one contact point

Every engagement starts with understanding what you are making and ends with documented, scalable flavour supply.

1

New product flavour development

You tell us the application, target profile, and regulatory market. We develop and iterate until the flavour works in your real product, not just a lab beaker.

2

Flavour matching

Bring us a reference: a competitor product, a legacy formula you have lost the recipe for, a flavour your supplier discontinued. We match and supply without the source.

3

Natural conversion

Reformulate artificial or nature-identical flavours to EU 1334/2008 natural status, keeping the profile your customers already know.

4

Technical and label reworks

Fix off-notes, adjust stability for your process, shift from liquid to powder, remove allergens, or reformulate for a cleaner declaration.
