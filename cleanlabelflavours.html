Why Use Clean Label Flavours

  • To replace artificial or synthetic flavourings with natural-origin alternatives
  • To meet consumer demand for recognisable and simple ingredient lists
  • To support "free-from" product positioning (e.g. additive-free, E-number-free)
  • To restore or enhance taste in minimally processed or reformulated foods
  • To support product claims like "natural flavouring" or "clean label compliant"
  • To achieve consistent flavour profile using EU 1334/2008 compliant sources
  • To maintain taste in reduced salt, fat or sugar applications

We supply food flavours in accordance with 1334/2008/EU regulation.
